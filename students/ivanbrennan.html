Ivan Brennan

I'm mathematically-minded, artistic, and I have a keen sense of the place where inspiration and collaboration meet.

Aspirations

I want to apply my understanding of mathematics to creative work. I like designing and constructing/deconstructing things and developing a comprehensive understanding of the mechanisms and logical connections involved. I have a deep appreciation for elegant solutions, both in terms of functionality and aesthetics. I believe I have the makings of an excellent programmer, as well as the passion for the work.

Interests

I'm a guitarist and composer, and for several years I actively pursued a career in music. The most powerful performances I played in were collaborative sessions where the seeds of a few ideas grew into something larger than any of us had anticipated. As ideas took root, a dialogue developed between the instruments, and the music acquired a palpable presence. While each player viewed it through his own lens, it was clear in the textured rhythms and the intertwining phrases, that we were immersed in a shared creative experience.
