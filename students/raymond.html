Oh, hello, I didn't see you there...

Born on a cold winter day in Donetsk, Ukraine, I have since called Miami, Detroit, Atlanta and New York, my home. I started working at the age of eight and since then I've been a dog walker, pool girl, smoothie maker, hostess, disaster recovery consultant and an auditor. In April of last year I left my job in search of greener pastures and found them in web development.
